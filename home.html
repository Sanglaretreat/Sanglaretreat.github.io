About Us

Begin your amazing adventure.

Nestled in the heart of the Baspa Valley, Sangla serves as a prime destination for adventurers and trekking enthusiasts. Its strategic location makes it the perfect base for exploring the surrounding landscapes and embarking on various trekking routes.

With its picturesque setting, Sangla offers easy access to the majestic beauty of the valley, surrounded by lush forests, snow-capped peaks, and charming villages.

Some of the Must-Visit Attractions and Treks in the Baspa Valley:

  • Kamru Fort
  • Lake and Dam, Kupa
  • Bering Nag Temple, Sangla
  • Batseri
  • Mata Devi Temple Chhitkul
  • Piri Nages Temple, Sapni
  • Rupin Valley Trek
  • Sangla Kande Trek
  • Charang Chitkul Trek
